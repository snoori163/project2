What makes these benefits valuable

SIMPLICITY. We are aiming to simplify a process that currently takes most people a long time to do. Food preparation can take very long, especially when it’s required to portion out your fruits, vegetables, meats, etc., and current solutions only remedy the problem with a lot of “guess and check” style estimations of macronutrients. We want to just deliver fast and accurate nutrient information. It’s a simple yet humble mission.

Customer's main problem

Customers are currently facing a tedious and complex process of measuring and calculating macronutrients and micronutrients in their food intake everyday.

Why we are better

Existing apps require the user to input serving size, food's type. Our product will take care of that. The user will put the food on the scale, and the scale will calculate nutrients' facts and that’s what differentiate us.
